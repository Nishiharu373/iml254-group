Warranty

Please read more about the warranties provided below:
PHONE/TABLET

Phone Warranties

Manufacturer Warranty:
Covers manufacturing defects, hardware malfunctions, and software issues.
Typically lasts 1 year.

Extended Warranty:
Extends the coverage beyond the standard warranty period.
May include additional services like free repairs or replacements.

Accidental Damage Protection:
Covers physical damages like cracked screens or liquid spills.
Offered as an add-on service.

Battery Warranty:
Covers defective batteries (limited cycles).May last for 6-12 months.

Screen Protection Plan:
Covers screen replacement for accidental damage.
Often limited to one replacement per year.

LAPTOP

Laptop Warranties

Manufacturer Warranty:
Covers hardware failures, including motherboard, display, and keyboard.Typically lasts 1 year.

Extended Warranty:
Provides longer coverage for hardware defects.Offered by the manufacturer or third-party providers.

Accidental Damage Protection:
Covers drops, spills, and other physical damages.

Battery and Adapter Warranty:
Covers battery malfunctions and defective chargers.May be limited to 6-12 months.

International Warranty:
Allows servicing in different countries, ideal for frequent travelers.

Software Support Warranty:
Offers support for OS-related issues or pre-installed applications.

TELEVISION

TV Warranties

Manufacturer Warranty:
Covers defects in display, power supply, and other hardware.
Typically lasts 1-2 years.

Extended Warranty:
Extends coverage for additional years (up to 5 years).
Covers issues like display failure or motherboard issues.

Pixel Warranty:
Covers dead pixels or burn-in on LED, OLED, or QLED screens.

On-Site Warranty:
Includes at-home repairs or servicing.
Common for larger TVs.

Power Surge Protection:
Covers damage caused by electrical surges.
